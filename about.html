Making Recycling Seamless, Step by Step

At RecycleHub, our process is designed to be effortless for users and effective for the planet. From your doorstep to our eco-centers, each step reflects our promise of transparency, efficiency, and impact.

1. Pickup Scheduling
Users can easily schedule waste pickups through our app or website. We ensure timely collection with route optimization to reduce carbon emissions.

2. Safe Collection
Our trained collection team ensures proper handling and categorization of materials at the source, preventing contamination and maximizing recyclability.

3. Sorting at Recovery Centers
Collected items are sorted by material type (plastic, paper, metal, e-waste, etc.) using semi-automated and manual sorting for higher precision and recovery.

4. Eco-Friendly Preprocessing
Waste is cleaned, dried, shredded, or composted depending on its type. Water used in cleaning is treated and reused in-house.

5. Repurposing & Recycling
The cleaned materials are sent for recycling or upcycling ‚Äî turned into usable products like recycled boards, bricks, furniture, packaging and more.

6. Re-Entry into the Market
